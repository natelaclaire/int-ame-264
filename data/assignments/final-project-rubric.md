### **Total Grade Breakdown:**

* **A (90-100%)**: Excellent work, deep and thoughtful analysis, highly organized and polished.  
* **B (80-89%)**: Good work, covers most areas well, but with minor issues in depth or clarity.  
* **C (70-79%)**: Satisfactory, covers the basics but lacks depth or has organizational and clarity issues.  
* **D (60-69%)**: Needs improvement, minimal analysis and poorly organized.  
* **F (\<60%)**: Fails to meet expectations or criteria in most areas.
