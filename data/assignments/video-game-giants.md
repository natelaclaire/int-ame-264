# Video Game Giants

Each student will select a prominent figure from the video game industry—such as a designer, artist, writer, programmer, composer, hardware developer, or another key contributor—and create an informative profile of that individual. The profile should explore the individual’s impact on the industry and provide personal insights into why you chose them. This assignment is self-paced \- it can be completed at any time throughout the course.

## **The Profile Should Include:**

* A brief introduction to the individual and their background.  
* Key contributions to the video game industry (major works, innovations, or influence).  
* Personal insights explaining why you selected this figure.  
* Any additional interesting facts or stories about their career or legacy.

**Format and Submission:**

* **Length**: Written profiles should be 500-750 words. Alternatively, you can create a **multimedia presentation** (video, PowerPoint, etc.) that lasts 5-7.5 minutes.  
* **Citations:** Any sources used should be cited in some format (especially for quotes or lesser-known facts).  
* **Submission**: Each student needs to submit one profile during the course at the time of the student’s choice. Profiles will be posted to a discussion forum when ready.  
* **Engagement**: Students are expected to actively engage by reading and responding to profiles. Share your thoughts and ask questions. Thoughtful engagement will contribute to your participation grade.

The goal is to foster an ongoing conversation about the key players who have shaped the gaming world while allowing you to explore individuals whose work inspires or interests you.
