### **Grading Scale:**

* **90-100% (A):** Outstanding work that meets or exceeds all expectations. Demonstrates deep critical thinking, thorough reflection, and clear connections to course material.  
* **80-89% (B):** Strong work that fulfills most of the assignment’s requirements with clear reflection and connection to course material, but lacks depth in some areas.  
* **70-79% (C):** Adequate work that meets the basic requirements but lacks depth, clarity, or strong connections to the course material.  
* **60-69% (D):** Incomplete or superficial work that does not fully meet the assignment criteria or shows limited engagement with the course material.  
* **\<60% (F):** Unsatisfactory work that fails to meet the assignment requirements or demonstrate meaningful engagement with the course content.
